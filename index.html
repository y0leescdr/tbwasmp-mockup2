2. The Action Plan: Tailored Use Cases

The heatmap identifies the "where." To help the strategy team brainstorm, here are a few tailored examples of the "what"—turning data into a concrete action plan for their specific clients.

Use Case for JTI
Target: Young Adult Market

Key Drivers

  • Foot Traffic: High density of adult students (2 universities nearby).
  • Competition: Low saturation of formal convenience stores in adjacent side-streets.
  • Demographics: Area dominated by young professionals (3 BPO centers).
  • Accessibility: Served by a major transit hub.

💡 Insight

This area shows a high concentration of your target demographic with low competitor visibility in the specific stores they frequent after class or work.

🎯 Suggested Strategic Action

Deploy a "Trade Blitz Team" to these hotspots to ensure 100% stock availability and deploy compliant point-of-sale materials.

Use Case for Del Monte
Target: Family Meal Planning

Key Drivers

  • Foot Traffic: High density of shoppers near a major public market.
  • Competition: Underserved by major supermarkets.
  • Demographics: Area is a dense residential subdivision with a high concentration of families.
  • Accessibility: Main tricycle routes pass through the area.

💡 Insight

This area is frequented by families doing their weekly grocery shopping, making sari-sari stores a key channel for top-up purchases for home-cooked meals.

🎯 Suggested Strategic Action

Run a "Bundling Promo" in these hotspots. Offer a discount on Del Monte Tomato Sauce when purchased with pasta.

Use Case for Alaska
Target: Neighborhood Breakfast

Key Drivers

  • Foot Traffic: High density of students and parents (4 elementary schools nearby).
  • Competition: The furthest residential zone from any major supermarket in the barangay.
  • Demographics: Area dominated by families with young children.
  • Accessibility: Multiple internal subdivision roads, less accessible to large delivery trucks.

💡 Insight

This residential hotspot is underserved by major supermarkets, making local sari-sari stores the primary source for daily needs like milk for breakfast ("baon" and coffee).

🎯 Suggested Strategic Action

Launch a "Neighborhood Nutrition" campaign. Ensure perfect placement and stock of Alaska products in these stores.
